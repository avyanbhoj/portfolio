Cricket:

My passion for cricket began when I was just 8 years old. Every day, I play the sport with my friends in the parks and cricket ground in our society. As a child, cricket was all around me as my family were very passionate about the sport. Following their footsteps, I soon learnt how to play it and developed an interest in it. Now, it is one of my favorite sports to watch and play! My favorite cricket teams are India and Gujarat Titans and my favorite cricketer is Shubman Gill.
