Mekathlon 2024: An International Science & Robotics Competition

I joined a robotics lab in Mumbai called TMCL or MakerWorks. Through the lab, we created a robotics team and participated in Mekathlon, a robotics tournament. We first participated in the city level. We came 2nd place and qualified for the International Grand Finale in Bangalore where many different countries participated. We emerged victorious as we bagged a bronze medal. It was a very fun and lovely experience.
